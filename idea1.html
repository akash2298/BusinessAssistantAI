Business Ideation

  • Products and services that are on the market and generate sales have something in common, they either solve a problem, provide a solution to a need or satisfy a passion Therefore, the first thing you have to focus on is finding a need to solve, a solution to offer, or a passion to satisfy in the market.
  • The creative process is the evolution of an idea into its final form through a progression of thoughts and actions. This space will help you with this process by developing brainstorming sessio ns. Based on the necessities, solutions or passions identify in the previous step you can implement brainstorming sessions to land on features, characteristics, or updates to build your final business idea.
  • After carrying out the creative sessions, it is necessary to filter. This means, creating discussion to land on an idea that is feasible and for which you have the necessary resources. In this stage, it is helpful to land on three final ideas, features, or actions and then choose the one that is more suitable.

  • Put here a summary (a paragraph) of your final business idea. Remember to answer the questions:

    1. a. What need do we solve?
    2. b. Who is this product for?
    3. c. Is a service, a product or both?
    4. d. Highlight the value and features.
